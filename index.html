About Deutsch Scholar

Deutsch Scholar is an educational consultancy and e-Learning Language firm (classroom & Online) set-up with the basic running operation of providing or helping African International students to further their studies in developed countries and pursue a career pathway in their huge employment pool by undergoing an intensive German language course recognized my International and National institutions.

Deutsch Scholar also helps children who do not have access to education to learn the language and engage in professional or vocational studies necessary to create an enabling future.

This would in turn promote the educational standard in Africa and decrease labor shortages caused by demographic decline in Germany.

We will at all times demonstrate undivided commitment to sustainability, both individually and as a firm, by actively participating in our communities and integrating sustainable business practices wherever possible.
